PG ARES is a field service organization of the Amateur Radio Relay League (ARRL). Membership requires include a valid FCC amateur radio license as well as a willingness to participate. Our main function is to serve the community with our Amateur Radio hobby in times of need.

To be an ARES Member:

1. Download and complete the membership application [at this link](/assets/files/2026-01-11PRGE-ARESmembershipForm.pdf).
2. Participate in our weekly on-air nets, Tuesdays at 7:30 PM ET.
3. Attend the monthly meetings, held on the 3rd Monday of each month at 7:00 PM ET.
4. Complete the FEMA Online following courses:
   1. [IS-100.C](https://training.fema.gov/is/courseoverview.aspx?code=IS-100.c&lang=en): Introduction to the Incident Command System (ICS 100)
   2. [IS-200.C](https://training.fema.gov/is/courseoverview.aspx?code=IS-200.c&lang=en): Basic Incident Command System for Initial Response (ICS-200)
   3. [IS-700.B](https://training.fema.gov/is/courseoverview.aspx?code=IS-700.b&lang=en): An Introduction to the National Incident Management System
   4. [IS-800.D](https://training.fema.gov/is/courseoverview.aspx?code=IS-800.d&lang=en): National Response Framework, an Introduction
5. Send your course completion certificates and membership forms to kb3sph [at] arrl.net
